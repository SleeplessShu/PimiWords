Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2026

Pimi ("we", "our", or "the app") is a vocabulary learning application. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your personal information.

1. Data We Collect

  • Google account information — name and email address, collected when you sign in with Google to enable cloud sync
  • User dictionary and learning progress — word groups, custom words, and game statistics you create inside the app
  • Crash reports — anonymous technical data collected via Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To sync your dictionary and progress across devices
  • To associate your data with your account so it is not lost
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app stability

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

Pimi uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, cloud storage, crash reporting
  • Google Sign-In — account authentication

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Firebase servers operated by Google. We apply Firebase security rules to ensure that each user can only access their own data. We do not have access to your Google account password.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as you use the app. If you wish to delete your data, please contact us at the email address below and we will remove it within 30 days.

Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device. Cloud data (dictionary, progress) remains on Firebase until you request deletion.

6. Children

Pimi is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent to data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
